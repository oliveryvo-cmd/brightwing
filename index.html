How BrightWing Works

Two sides. One mission.
🙋
Sign Up
If you have a disability, sign up and add yourself to the BrightWing map. Tell the world who you are.

People all over the world can see you on the BrightWing map and choose to send you a surprise box.

A mystery box shows up at your door — packed with handmade slime, toys, a cheer-up note, and more.
