Questions?

Everything you want to know about BrightWing, answered by the founder.

What is BrightWing?
BrightWing is The Joy Box Company — we connect kids with disabilities to people who want to cheer them up. You pick someone, order a joy box, and we deliver a surprise straight to their door. Founded by Oliver Vo, age 10, who has Tourette syndrome and knows exactly how much a surprise can change your day.
Who can sign up to receive boxes?
Anyone with a disability! Tourette's, ADHD, autism, cerebral palsy, anything — visible or invisible. You only share what you're comfortable sharing. If you're under 18, ask a parent first.
How much does a box cost?
There are four boxes: Mini Wing ($15), Joy Box ($30), Big Wing Box ($50), and the Ultimate Wing Box ($100). Check out The Boxes page to see everything inside each one.
Where do you deliver?
Right now: Ladera Ranch, California — Oliver delivers every box personally, by bike. But check out the Flight Plan on the home page: Orange County is next, then all of California, then the world. 🌍
How do I pay?
Cash on delivery. You don't pay anything online — you pay when Oliver shows up at your door with the box. Simple and safe.
Who packs the boxes?
Oliver packs every single box himself, and writes every note by hand. The slime is handmade too. No two boxes are ever the same — every one is a surprise.
I don't live nearby. How can I help?
Join the BrightWing Crew! You can spread the word, and when BrightWing reaches your city, you could become a City Captain — the person who packs and delivers joy boxes in YOUR neighborhood.
Is BrightWing a real company?
Yes! BrightWing is part of D.O.V.E LLC, founded by Oliver Vo. Oliver has started 11 companies — but BrightWing is the one built to change lives.
